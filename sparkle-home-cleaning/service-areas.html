House Cleaning in Daytona Beach, FL

As our home base, Daytona Beach is where Sparkle Home Cleaning got its start, and it's still where we clean the most homes. From beachside condos along A1A to family homes near LPGA Boulevard and the historic neighborhoods around Beach Street, we understand the unique cleaning needs of coastal Florida living.

Sand, humidity, and salt air mean Daytona Beach homes need more frequent dusting and floor care than inland properties, and our team knows exactly how to keep up with it. Whether you live in a high-rise condo or a single-family home near Riverfront Park, we tailor each visit to your space.

House Cleaning in Ormond Beach, FL

Known as the "Birthplace of Speed," Ormond Beach blends historic charm with quiet, tree-lined residential streets. We regularly clean homes throughout the Tomoka neighborhoods, along the Halifax River, and in the established communities west of US-1.

Many Ormond Beach homes we clean are older properties with hardwood floors, detailed trim work, and mature landscaping that tracks in more dirt and pollen. Our team adjusts our approach to protect original flooring and fixtures while keeping every room spotless.

House Cleaning in Holly Hill, FL

Holly Hill is a small, tight-knit community tucked between Daytona Beach and Ormond Beach, known for its mix of starter homes, bungalows, and long-term rentals. We work with both homeowners and landlords here who want dependable, affordable cleaning without sacrificing quality.

Our Holly Hill clients frequently choose our Standard Clean package for recurring upkeep, and our Move In / Move Out service for rental turnovers along Ridgewood Avenue and the surrounding streets.
