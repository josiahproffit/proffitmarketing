House Cleaning on Amelia Island, FL

Amelia Island is where Amelia Shine Cleaning Services got its start, cleaning vacation rentals and beach homes for friends before growing into a full residential and commercial cleaning company. It's still where we clean the most homes today, from oceanfront condos to homes tucked into the island's quiet, moss-draped neighborhoods.

Sand, humidity, and salt air mean island homes need more frequent dusting and floor care than inland properties, and short-term rental turnovers need to happen fast and right the first time. Our team knows exactly how to keep up with both, whether you own a full-time residence or a vacation property you rent out between visits.

House Cleaning in Fernandina Beach, FL

Fernandina Beach's historic downtown, with its Victorian storefronts along Centre Street and working shrimp docks on the river, sits alongside quiet residential streets full of century-old homes and newer builds near Fort Clinch. We regularly clean both, adjusting our approach for older hardwood floors and detailed trim work as carefully as we do for modern kitchens and baths.

As the seat of Nassau County, Fernandina Beach is home to many of our longest-standing recurring clients, families who've trusted the same cleaning team for years.

House Cleaning in Yulee, FL

Yulee is one of the fastest-growing communities in Nassau County, with new subdivisions and master-planned neighborhoods like Wildlight bringing in families who commute toward Jacksonville along I-95 and A1A. We see a lot of busy, dual-income households here with growing kids and packed schedules.

That's exactly why recurring residential cleaning is so popular in Yulee. Our weekly and biweekly plans help local families keep up with larger, newer homes without giving up their weekends.

House Cleaning in Callahan, FL

Callahan is a small, tight-knit community in western Nassau County, known for its rural, close-to-the-land character, larger properties, and a slower pace than the coast. We work with homeowners here who want the same dependable, detail-driven cleaning we bring to homes on the island.

Our Callahan clients frequently choose our Standard Clean package for recurring upkeep, along with our Move In / Move Out service for families relocating in or out of the area.

House Cleaning in Hilliard, FL

Tucked near the Georgia border in far western Nassau County, Hilliard offers a quiet, rural alternative to the busier coast. It's a close-knit community of families, farms, and long-time residents who value a cleaning team they can trust to show up and do the job right.
