Our Story

Getting to Know Amelia Shine Cleaning Services

A locally owned cleaning company built on trust, consistency, and genuine care for the homes of Amelia Island.

How We Started

A Cleaner's Eye for Detail, Since 1995

Anna Santana has been cleaning professionally since 1995, with more than 20 years of hands-on experience behind her. Her start was simple: cleaning residential vacation rentals and Airbnbs on Amelia Island for friends who wanted someone they could trust between guests.

As word spread and demand grew, Anna turned that trust into a business, Amelia Shine Cleaning Services. What began with a handful of vacation properties has grown into a full-service cleaning company serving homes and businesses across Amelia Island and Nassau County.

Today, Amelia Shine provides residential cleaning, commercial cleaning, construction cleanup, and move-in / move-out cleaning, but the standard hasn't changed since day one: treat every space like it's our own.
