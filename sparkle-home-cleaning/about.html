Our Story

Getting to Know Sparkle Home Cleaning

A locally owned cleaning company built on trust, consistency, and genuine care for the homes of Daytona Beach.

How We Started

A One-Woman Operation That Grew Into a Trusted Local Team

Sparkle Home Cleaning began in 2015 when founder Maria Sanchez started cleaning homes for a handful of families in her own Daytona Beach neighborhood. Word of mouth spread quickly, homeowners loved coming home to a spotless house, and the referrals kept coming.

Ten years later, Sparkle Home Cleaning has grown into a full team of trained, background-checked cleaning specialists serving Daytona Beach, Ormond Beach, Port Orange, Holly Hill, and South Daytona. What hasn't changed is the standard we hold ourselves to: treat every home like it's our own.

We're still locally owned, still locally operated, and still personally invested in every home we clean.
