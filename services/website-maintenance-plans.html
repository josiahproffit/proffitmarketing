Website Maintenance Plans

A site that isn't maintained slowly drifts out of date — software gets outdated, small errors go unnoticed, and content stops reflecting the business. Maintenance is built into every plan rather than sold as an occasional emergency service, so small issues get caught before they turn into a redesign.

That also covers the small, ongoing changes every business needs — updating hours, adding a new service, swapping a photo — without needing a developer or a new invoice every time.

What's included

Regular software and security updates
Small content edits included — hours, pricing, photos, text changes
Monitoring for broken links, forms, or errors
Monthly SEO and analytics review
Direct access to request changes, not a ticket system
No separate invoice for routine maintenance

Why it matters

Sites that go untouched for a year or more are far more likely to have outdated information, broken forms nobody noticed, or security vulnerabilities — all of which quietly cost leads. Ongoing maintenance catches these before a customer does.
