Small Business Web Design

Small business owners don't need an enterprise marketing platform, they need a site that answers the phone, so to speak: it shows up in local search, explains what you do clearly, and makes it easy for someone to reach out. That's the entire design brief, and it's exactly what we build.

We work almost exclusively with small, local, owner-operated businesses across Northeast Florida, which means the process is built around how you actually operate, no marketing jargon, no long onboarding forms, no agency retainer.

What's included

A site sized correctly for a small business, not an over-built platform you'll never fully use
Plans starting at $399 to design and launch, from $97/mo afterward, no surprise invoices

Direct access to the person who built your site, not a support ticket queue
Local SEO basics included from the start

A lot of small business owners either overpay for an agency package built for much larger companies, or underinvest in a DIY builder that never quite gets finished.
