Website Migration

Business owners often stay on a website platform they've outgrown, an expensive agency contract, a DIY builder they're tired of, simply because moving feels risky. Done correctly, migrating your domain, content, and rankings to a new, better-built site carries far less risk than most owners expect.

We review what you currently have, keep your domain and any content that's working, and rebuild the site properly with a redirect plan so search engines and existing links transfer over correctly.

What's included

A full review of your current site before migrating anything
Your domain moved and configured correctly, with no downtime
Existing content and rankings preserved through a proper 301 redirect plan
A rebuilt site on faster, more reliable infrastructure
Cancellation guidance for your previous platform or agency, if needed
Ongoing hosting and care afterward, so you're not migrating again in a year

Why it matters

The single biggest risk in switching platforms is losing existing Google rankings because URLs change with no redirect plan in place, this is avoidable with proper planning, but it's the step most DIY migrations skip. Getting it right means the switch is a clean upgrade, not a step backward.

How it works

1

Review your current site

2

Rebuild & map redirects

The new site is built while every existing URL is mapped to its new home.

3

Cut over with zero downtime

Your domain is pointed to the new site with redirects live from day one.
